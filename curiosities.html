Some curiosities and Easter eggs from the series

The Citadel of Ricks

The Citadel of Ricks is a society formed by thousands of alternate versions of Rick and Morty. What begins as a refuge quickly becomes a mirror of real political systems, revealing class inequality, corruption and control within the multiverse.

The Portal Gun

Rick’s portal gun allows instant travel between dimensions using its iconic green portals. Its existence reinforces Rick’s god-like intelligence, while also symbolizing the lack of limits—or responsibility—in his actions.

The Cronenberg Dimension

After a failed experiment, an entire universe mutates into grotesque creatures. Rather than fixing the chaos, Rick abandons the dimension, exposing the show’s dark humor and its recurring theme: consequences are optional.

Mr. Meeseeks

Mr. Meeseeks are creatures created for a single purpose. Once their task is complete, they disappear. Their desperation to end their own existence turns a simple joke into an unsettling reflection on meaning and suffering.

The Microverse Battery

Rick powers his spaceship using an entire miniature universe. Inside that universe exists another one, creating a chain of exploited realities that critiques capitalism and blind scientific ambition.

Evil Morty

Evil Morty represents a break from Rick’s dominance. Intelligent, calm and strategic, he exposes the cruelty behind Rick’s genius and challenges the idea that Rick is always the smartest being in the room.
