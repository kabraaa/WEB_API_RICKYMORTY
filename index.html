What is Rick and Morty about?

Rick and Morty is an animated series that follows the adventures of Rick, a brilliant and alcoholic scientist, and his grandson Morty, an insecure teenager, as they travel through parallel universes and alternate realities filled with chaos, humor, and extreme science.

Humor, science, and chaos

The series is known for its biting humor, absurd situations, and sharp social commentary. Rick and Morty uses science fiction as a pretext to explore themes such as existence, family, morality, and the meaning of life, always with an irreverent and provocative tone.

A multiverse of characters

One of Rick and Morty's greatest strengths is its wide array of characters. From alternate versions of the protagonists to creatures from other universes, each character brings depth and personality to an unpredictable and ever-expanding multiverse.
